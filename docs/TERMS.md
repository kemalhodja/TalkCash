# TalkCash Terms and Subscription Notes

**Last updated:** June 2026

TalkCash is a personal finance and productivity application. It is not a bank, investment advisor, tax advisor, or licensed financial institution.

## Use of the App

- Users are responsible for the accuracy of transactions, receipts, budgets, and shared workspace data.
- AI-generated insights are informational and should not be treated as financial, legal, investment, or tax advice.
- Users must not upload unlawful content or data they are not authorized to process.

## Premium Plans

Premium features may include advanced analytics, AI finance coaching, unlimited or increased OCR usage, advanced reports, family workspaces, and small-business workflows.

During internal testing, premium plans may be activated through an internal endpoint for validation. Before public release, premium activation must be connected to Google Play Billing or another approved in-app purchase provider.

## Subscription, Cancellation, and Refunds

For Google Play distribution, subscription purchase, cancellation, renewal, and refund handling must follow Google Play policies. Users can manage subscriptions from their Google Play account.

## Shared Workspaces

Workspace owners are responsible for inviting members and controlling access. Members may see financial data shared in that workspace according to their role.

## Availability

Cloud services, AI processing, OCR, sync, and push notifications may be unavailable during maintenance or third-party provider outages.

## Account Deletion

Users can request account deletion from the app settings. Deletion removes account-linked app data controlled by TalkCash, subject to retention required for legal, billing, security, and abuse-prevention purposes.
