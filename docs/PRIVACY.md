# TalkCash Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

TalkCash collects and processes data necessary to provide personal finance management:

- **Account:** email, name, hashed password
- **Financial data:** wallets, transactions, budgets, bills, shopping lists
- **Optional:** receipt images, push notification tokens, location (geofencing, if enabled)
- **Voice:** audio sent for transcription when you use voice input

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on secure servers. You may delete your account from Settings.

Contact: support@talkcash.io (replace with your support email)
