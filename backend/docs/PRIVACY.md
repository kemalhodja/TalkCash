# TalkCash Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

TalkCash helps users manage personal finance, budgets, receipts, reminders, AI insights, shared workspaces, and optional small-business workflows.

## Data We Collect

- **Account data:** email, name, hashed password, locale, timezone, security settings.
- **Financial data:** wallets, transactions, categories, budgets, bills, shopping lists, debts, shared wallet records, workspace data.
- **Receipt data:** receipt images and OCR text when you scan or upload receipts.
- **Voice data:** audio is processed only when you start voice input.
- **AI usage data:** prompts, chat history, generated insights, and usage counters for premium limits.
- **Device and notification data:** push token, notification preferences, optional location/geofence state.
- **Product analytics:** feature usage events such as onboarding, premium paywall, insights, exports, OCR, and sync status.

## How We Use Data

- Provide account login, security lock, sync, reports, OCR, AI insights, and notifications.
- Enforce free/premium limits and subscription entitlements.
- Improve reliability, detect errors, and understand which product flows need improvement.
- Support account deletion, export, and customer support requests.

## Sharing

Data is not sold. We use trusted service providers only to operate the app, such as:

- **Hosting and database** (e.g. Render, PostgreSQL, Redis)
- **AI and speech** (e.g. OpenAI for NLP, transcription, and insights)
- **Push notifications** (device push token delivery)
- **Crash and error reporting** (e.g. Sentry, when enabled)

Only the minimum data needed for each feature is sent to these providers.

## Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed. Mobile sessions use secure storage. Premium and shared workspace actions are protected by server-side authorization and audit logs.

## Retention and Deletion

You may delete your account from Settings. Account deletion removes user data and receipt files controlled by TalkCash storage. Some billing, audit, or legal records may be retained when required for fraud prevention, compliance, or dispute handling.
